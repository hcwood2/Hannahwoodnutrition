About Me

I’m Hannah Wood, a Certified Nutrition Specialist (CNS) based in San Antonio, Texas. My practice is built around a functional approach that looks beyond symptoms to uncover the underlying causes of hormone imbalances, gut issues, chronic conditions and mental health challenges. Instead of cookie‑cutter diets, I craft research‑backed plans that fit your real life. Virtual telehealth appointments let us work together wherever you are, and optional testing helps us dig deeper into hormones, digestion and nutrient status so you can finally feel like yourself again.

Services I Offer

Hormone Nutrition

Support for period problems, endometriosis, thyroid issues, pre‑diabetes and diabetes to help you balance hormones and energy.

Gut Nutrition

Relief from bloating, gas, IBS, GERD, IBD, SIBO, celiac, gastroparesis and Crohn’s disease through targeted dietary and lifestyle strategies.

Chronic Conditions

Guidance for rheumatoid/psoriatic arthritis, Hashimoto’s thyroiditis, non‑alcoholic fatty liver disease, histamine intolerance and MCAS.

Mental Health Nutrition

Nutrition strategies to support anxiety, depression, ADHD and intuitive eating so you can nourish both body and mind.
